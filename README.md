# Kaecilius Ruin ⚔️🐉

A text-based fantasy RPG built in Java where players battle through Mount Ruin to defeat **Kaecilius, the Dragon King**.

The game features multiple archetypes, mana and HP systems, boss battles, defensive mechanics, ultimate abilities, and randomized enemy attacks.

---

## Gameplay

You begin as the Great Warrior, humanity’s final hope against Kaecilius and his corrupted forces.

Choose one of three archetypes:

* **Shadowblade** — agile assassin focused on dodging and burst damage
* **Runeseer** — powerful spellcaster with mana control and time manipulation
* **Warden** — defensive tank specializing in blocking and counterattacks

Fight through:

1. **Ravager Thorne** — Kaecilius’ elite gatekeeper
2. **Kaecilius, Dragon King of Ruin** — the final boss

---

## ⚔️ Archetypes

### Shadowblade

Fast assassin archetype focused on evasiveness and high burst damage.

| Ability       | Effect                          |
| ------------- | ------------------------------- |
| Shadow Strike | Deals 40 damage                 |
| Smoke Dash    | Dodges next attack              |
| Backstab      | Bonus damage if enemy HP is low |

---

### Runeseer

Mana-focused spellcaster with utility and battlefield control.

| Ability         | Effect             |
| --------------- | ------------------ |
| Arcane Pulse    | Deals magic damage |
| Mana Infusion   | Restores mana      |
| Time Distortion | Skips enemy turn   |

---

### Warden

Heavy defensive archetype with survivability and retaliation.

| Ability     | Effect                   |
| ----------- | ------------------------ |
| Iron Slam   | Heavy physical attack    |
| Shield Wall | Blocks next attack       |
| Vengeance   | Reflects previous damage |

---

## 🐉 Bosses

### Ravager Thorne

The corrupted guardian of Mount Ruin wielding molten attacks and mana-draining abilities.

### Kaecilius

The Dragon King himself. A high-damage final boss with destructive dragon magic and multiple combat phases.
